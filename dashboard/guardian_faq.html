Frequently Asked Questions

Hello Guardians,
Welcome to the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) section. This section is dedicated to answering the most commonly asked questions from both users and administrators. Whether you have a query about the application features, troubleshooting issues, or best practices, you'll find helpful information here

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I view daily reports?
To view daily reports, navigate to the Reports section and select the desired report from the list. Use the "View" button to open and review the details.
Can I edit the information in a report after it's been submitted?
Yes, you can edit the information in a submitted report. Simply click the "Edit" button next to the report you wish to modify and make the necessary changes.
How do I start a chat with the companion?
To start a chat with the companion, simply click on the "Chat" button in the application. This will open a chat window where you can type your queries or requests.
What do the different report statuses mean?
Completed: The report has been fully filled out and submitted.
Pending: The report is partially filled out and requires further input.
In Progress: The report is currently being worked on but has not been submitted yet.
Is the chat with the companion confidential?
For details on how your data is handled and the confidentiality of chats, please refer to Guarding Aging Youth's privacy statement here.
How do I request specific assistance from the companion?
You can request specific assistance by typing your request directly into the chat window. The companion will process your request and provide the necessary information or guidance.
What is the emergency contact for SOS situations?
The emergency contact is a designated person or service that you can reach out to in case of an emergency. This contact is intended to provide immediate assistance and ensure the safety and well-being of the individual under care. Make sure to keep this contact information updated and easily accessible in the application.
