FAQ Page
Frequently Asked Questions

Hi Medical Consultants,
Welcome to the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) section. This section is dedicated to answering the most commonly asked questions from guardians. Whether you have a query about medical consultations, chatting with the Medical Consultant, or best practices for elderly care, you'll find helpful information here.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a chat with the Medical Consultant?
To start a chat with the Medical Consultant, simply click on the "Chat" button in the application. This will open a chat window where you can type your queries or requests.
Can I ask for medical advice through the chat?
Yes, you can ask for medical advice through the chat. The Medical Consultant is available to provide fast and reliable consultations regarding the health and well-being of your elderly loved ones.
Is the chat with the Medical Consultant confidential?
For details on how your data is handled and the confidentiality of chats, please refer to Guarding Aging Youth's privacy statement here.
How do I request specific medical assistance from the Medical Consultant?
You can request specific medical assistance by typing your request directly into the chat window. The Medical Consultant will process your request and provide the necessary information or guidance.
What is the emergency contact for SOS situations?
The emergency contact is a designated person or service that you can reach out to in case of an emergency. This contact is intended to provide immediate assistance and ensure the safety and well-being of the individual under care. Make sure to keep this contact information updated and easily accessible in the application.
How do I ensure the well-being of my elderly loved one?
Regularly monitor their health, ensure they take their medications on time, maintain a balanced diet, and keep them engaged in physical and mental activities. Feel free to reach out to the Medical Consultant for personalized advice and support.
How do I handle a medical emergency?
In case of a medical emergency, contact the emergency services immediately. You can also reach out to the designated emergency contact in the application for immediate assistance. Ensure that you have all necessary medical information readily available.
What should I do if I have concerns about my elderly loved one's health?
If you have any concerns about your elderly loved one's health, do not hesitate to contact the Medical Consultant through the chat. Provide detailed information about the symptoms or issues, and the Medical Consultant will guide you on the next steps.
