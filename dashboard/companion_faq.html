Hello Companions,
Welcome to the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) section. As your dedicated companion, I'm here to assist you with all your queries and provide the support you need. This section is designed to answer the most commonly asked questions from both users and administrators. Whether you're looking for information about application features, troubleshooting tips, or best practices, you'll find helpful insights here

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use real-time chat?
To initiate a real-time chat, simply click on the "Chat" button. This allows you to communicate instantly with other caregivers, administrators, or the elderly.
How do I view and submit daily reports?
Daily reports can be viewed and submitted through the Reports section. Select the appropriate report, fill in the necessary details, and submit it for review.
What happens when I click the Emergency SOS button?
When you click the Emergency SOS button, an alert is immediately sent to the designated emergency contact, and the relevant authorities if necessary. The location and any pertinent information about the emergency situation are also communicated to ensure quick response and assistance.
What do the different report statuses mean?
Completed: The report has been fully filled out and submitted.
Pending: The report is partially filled out and requires further input.
In Progress: The report is currently being worked on but has not been submitted yet.
Is the chat with the companion confidential?
For details on how your data is handled and the confidentiality of chats, please refer to Guarding Aging Youth's privacy statement here.
How do I change my personal details?
To change your personal details, navigate to the Profile section in the application. Here, you can update your contact information, address, and other personal details. Make sure to save the changes once you have updated the information
What should I do if I encounter a technical issue with the application?
If you encounter a technical issue, first check the FAQ section for troubleshooting tips. If the issue persists, contact the support team through the Support section in the application. Provide details about the problem, and our team will assist you promptly.
